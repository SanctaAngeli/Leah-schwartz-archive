## ABSTRACT
*trying to be trendy*

In the early sixties I had a brief fling with flinging. I smeared, spattered
and poured paint onto canvases spread on the floor. I had taken a class at
the San Francisco Art Institute from an instructor whose only advice was
let it happen, so I did. It was fun for awhile, but I don't think abstractly
and I don't worship paint for its own sake so I resumed painting what I
truly love: the real, funny, beautiful, ironic visual world.
