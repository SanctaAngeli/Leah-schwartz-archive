## LANDSCAPE
*vanishing verdant valleys*

I used to paint landscapes from my old Ford Econoline van that had an
engine housing between the front seats. The top of the housing was flat
and formed a convenient table for paints, and the steering wheel was at a
good angle for watercolor painting. In my portable studio I could even
paint in the rain.
