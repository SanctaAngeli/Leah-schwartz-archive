## INTERIORS
*gimme shelter*

From my mother I inherited a love of houses (God knows we lived in
a great number and variety of dwellings). For years I assumed this was an
ordinary and widespread interest shared by most women and some men.
I now know it's a ridiculous assumption. Most people don't give a damn
about southern exposures, covered entries, convenient kitchens, "traffic
flow," closet space, where the sun rises in the winter time, or whether a
room has enough corners. I do. I have a secret yearning to be an architect
and I designed the house we live in, converted our Bolinas barn-shack to a
farm house, and was delighted to be asked to design a house for my young
friend Lisa Adler who lives in the Sierras.
