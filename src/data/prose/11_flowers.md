## FLOWERS
*in love with bloom*

Most of my flower watercolors were painted from my husband's dazzling
flower garden on the edge of the bay with the San Francisco skyline for a
backdrop (which I have used in several paintings). A few years ago our dear
friend Lee Boltin, the photographer, introduced me to Andre Sala, the art
director of Portal Publications, a large poster and note card company with
headquarters near here. When I showed Andre some of my paintings, he said
they didn't use work like that and recommended an "Art Poster" firm in Los
Angeles. My husband said, "He's crazy," and went back awhile later with a
bunch of color slides and at that time Andre changed his mind and decided to
publish a few. The posters were so popular that Portal published a second
batch. Friends reported sightings in Britain, Belgium, France, Australia, and
all over the United States, including Alaska and Hawaii. I also get letters from
other Leahs and from people who have named their daughters Leah. That's
fun. When I was a kid I thought I was the only Leah in the world.
