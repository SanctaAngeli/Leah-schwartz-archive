# High Sierra

For two weeks in the summer we hike in the high country of
Desolation Valley. This is in an area just south of Lake Tahoe and a few
thousand feet higher, and anything but desolate. It is a miracle of white
granite boulders, spring flowers, crystal lakes, cascading streams and
occasionally a grassy, flower-spangled meadow. We rent a cabin on
Echo Lake, at the base of Desolation Valley, with our friends the
Adlers and Perloffs. The cabin can be reached by boat, or on foot over a
beautiful rocky trail. We hike all day and read to each other by kerosene
lamp at night.
